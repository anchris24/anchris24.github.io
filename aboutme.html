About Me

Nice to meet you!

As a second-year undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science & Engineering at MIT, my experiences lie in leveraging AI and machine learning within the intersection of computer science, mathematics, and business data analytics. My rigorous background in AI/ML include projects implementing large language models for a multimodal voice chatbot and employing explainable machine learning models to ascertain critical features in predicting toxicity in drug design components. These efforts, alongside my experience in communication and leadership, underscore my commitment to exploring creative incorporations of AI/ML technologies and uniquely position me to contribute to innovative work in AI-powered software.

My academic and extracurricular experiences have helped me build a strong analytical and problem-solving skillset. My prior coursework at MIT consists of rigorous computer science and mathematics, namely Machine Learning, Algorithms, and Probability & Random Variables. As a proactive learner, I continue to dive deeper, taking graduate-level Deep Learning, Design & Analysis of Algorithms, and Statistics in the upcoming semester. I have developed effective technical writing and oral/visual presentation skills, demonstrated by my ongoing work on multiple manuscripts and symposium research presentations. In addition, I volunteered multiple years as the director of INTEGIRLS, a nonprofit to empower minorities in STEM, where I strengthened my communication abilities, teamwork, and aptitude for working in multidisciplinary environments. This has nurtured me to become both a critical and creative thinker, leveraging novel tools to contribute to pioneering solutions while collaborating with diverse teams.

In my free time...

In between classes and extracurricular projects, I enjoy experimenting with different forms of arts & crafts, such as crocheting and needle felting! Other art forms I have explored include pottery, watercolor, and embroidery. These activities allow me to express my creativity and unwind from the rigors of academic life. I find joy in creating unique pieces and sharing them with loved ones, whether it's a glazed keychain for a friend or a cute felted animal to cheer up a family member.
